MoviesJoy Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to MoviesJoy (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "MoviesJoy"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how MoviesJoy collects, uses, stores, and safeguards your personal information, as well as your rights regarding privacy.

Information We Collect

MoviesJoy does not gather or retain any personal or non-personal information from its users. There is no need to register, and we do not request or handle data like names, email addresses, IP addresses, or browsing history.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a third-party analytics tool provided by Google, to better understand user interactions and enhance our website. Google Analytics may gather non-personal information, such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type
  • Device type
Google collects, processes, and stores this information in accordance with their Privacy Policy. MoviesJoy does not access or control the data collected by Google through its tools. To disable Google Analytics tracking, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Children's Privacy

MoviesJoy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe MoviesJoy has collected your child's information, please contact us.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

MoviesJoy may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will take effect upon posting the updated policy on MoviesJoy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at E-mail: movie2joys.online@gmail.com. We will respond to your inquiries as soon as possible. Thank you for using MoviesJoy.
